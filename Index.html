Our Founders: The Mavericks and Visionaries

Our Founders are mavericks, visionaries, the risk-takers, and the change-makers who dare to defy convention and chart their own path. They are not just entrepreneurs; they are revolutionaries who challenge the status quo, break boundaries, and inspire others to think differently. Their journeys are defined by bold decisions, unconventional ideas, and a deep belief in the power of innovation and creativity.

Turning Obstacles into Opportunities

We spot opportunities where others see obstacles and embrace the unknown with fearless determination. We like ideas that are disruptive and have the potential to transform entire markets or even create new ones. We come from diverse social and economic backgrounds and understand the struggles of creating economic and social value from an idea. Our hope is to inspire a generation of entrepreneurs to aim high and think big, irrespective of their position today.

Fostering Creativity and Thinking

The small team thrives on fostering creativity and unconventional thinking. We understand that in order to create something truly groundbreaking, ideas must be encouraged that defy traditional frameworks. Our founders believe in what Steve Jobs said: “Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower.” By focusing on design, simplicity, and user experience, we are able to create products that will revolutionize the industry we choose as our playground.

Building Communities That Dream Big

The hallmark of our founders is not just their success but their ability to inspire and empower others to follow their dreams. We don’t just build companies—we build communities of people who are motivated to push boundaries alongside us. We mentor and motivate countless individuals to pursue their passions, no matter how unconventional.

Failure: A Stepping Stone to Success

For us, failure is not a setback but an essential part of the journey to success. We redefine failure as a stepping stone to growth and encourage others to embrace it as a learning opportunity. Jeff Bezos says, “Failure and invention are inseparable twins.” We absolutely stand by the statement and encourage entrepreneurs to learn from their mistakes, iterate, and keep moving forward.

Beyond Profits: Creating Lasting Change

Who does not like profits? We genuinely do, but we aren’t just interested in profits—we want to create lasting change. The team believes that business success can be intertwined with a larger, global mission to make a difference.

Inspiring the Next Generation of Innovators

Our focus is to actively champion new generations of thinkers and doers. We genuinely believe that the next big idea could come from anyone, regardless of background or experience.
