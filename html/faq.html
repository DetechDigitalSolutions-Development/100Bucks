FAQs

Our ethos is simple: we believe that every idea is a good one, but that execution is the key to success. As a result, we invest in the people behind the idea, rather than the idea alone. You are in the right place if you believe you are the right founder and have a brilliant idea. Let's get started and explore.

Does 100 Bucks invest at seed?

Yes, we believe in the idea and the people behind the idea. We back people, their teams, and the idea long before the world has even understood the potential of the idea.

We have just invested in seed stage with an edtech company and we are actively mentoring the team to ensure that the business is a resolute success. We will not only invest at seed stage but also provide inputs and, if required, participate operationally to ensure the seed flowers into a plant.

We work with companies across four key stages: seed, venture, growth, and global growth.

What should I do next?

If you write to any one of us, you would have reached all of us. We share our networks and expertise. We will get back to you with our response in the shortest possible time and share our feedback.

How do I pitch it?

All you need to do is create a comprehensive and compelling deck on your idea. What we want you to capture is the problem the idea is solving for and details of the team which is going to deliver.

While pedigree is important, it is not the only barometer that we look for. What is important to us are the people who have a creative, disruptive idea and their passion to nurture the idea.

If we do not see eye-to-eye after the presentation, we are absolutely okay to hear your next idea. Our doors will always be open for you.

What does partnership mean to us?

We will invest in a select few businesses. We engage actively and will bring to the table our expertise, hand-hold, and guide you. We will not shy away from sharing our collective experience and passing it down to you without a hitch to ensure success is guaranteed.

We are relentless, sincere, and patient. We have made mistakes and learned from them. All the wealth of our experience will be at your disposal to ensure that you do not make the mistakes we made, saving on costs, reruns, and frustration.

Since we work with founders at every stage, you will see us stand right by your side every step of the way.
